None of us were expecting our lives to be disrupted due to the Covid-19 pandemic this year. Many of us are in a lockdown situation again, unable to continue what we have been doing, and unable to travel and see loved ones.

This mood board was made to record how we are coping during this time around the globe. You may leave your good and bad moods — your worries, hopes, and dreams. And perhaps while browsing others’, you will see that we are in this together.

Please feel free to send an email for any queries & suggestions.

How to leave your mood on the map

Simply go to this page to submit your mood. Note that your mood will not appear on the globe (but it will appear on the list view) if you block your geolocation when prompted.
